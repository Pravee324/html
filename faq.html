Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I submit a review?

You can submit a review by navigating to the "Submit Review" page, filling out the form, and clicking the submit button.

2. Can I edit or delete my reviews?

Currently, editing or deleting reviews is not supported in the system. Please contact support if you need assistance.

3. Is there a limit to the number of reviews I can submit?

No, you can submit as many reviews as you like for different universities.

4. How is my data protected?

Your data is stored locally and is not shared with third parties. We recommend not including sensitive information in your reviews.

5. Who can I contact for further questions?

You can reach us through the "Contact Us" page for any inquiries.
